About me

Introduction

Hi, I'm Rounak Kumar, a BCA student at VIT Vellore and an aspiring Full-Stack Software Developer. I have a strong passion for coding and problem-solving, with experience in C++, Python, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and MySQL.

Skills & Learning Journey

I've worked on projects like a login page, a web page, and an event description page, and I'm currently diving deep into frontend development—I've completed HTML & CSS, currently learning JavaScript, and will soon explore React.

Hobbies & Interests

Beyond coding, I enjoy watching movies, playing cricket, and riding. My goal is to build scalable and impactful digital solutions, continuously improving my skills along the way.

Projects

Project 1

A Create Account Page allows users to register by entering their name, email, password, and other details. It includes input validation and a secure registration process. Users can also log in using Google and Apple for a faster, hassle-free signup experience with just one click.

Project 2

A Login Page allows users to securely access their accounts by entering their email and password. It includes input validation and authentication. Users can also log in via Google, Facebook, LinkedIn, and GitHub. If they don’t have an account, they can register easily.

Project 3

A Job Finder Web Page helps users find the perfect job by providing personalized recommendations. Users can sign up or log in using Google, Facebook, LinkedIn, or GitHub. They can enter details like skills, experience, and preferences to get tailored job listings and career opportunities.

Project 4

A VIT Club Page showcases upcoming events with details on venue, speakers, and sponsors. Users can explore event schedules, speaker profiles, and sponsor contributions. The page offers a seamless experience to stay updated on club activities, registrations, and exclusive opportunities at VIT.
